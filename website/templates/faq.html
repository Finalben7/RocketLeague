Frequently Asked Questions

The Underground was created to bring exciting and meaningful season based competitions, that only high end organizations currently have access to, to the everyday ranked warriors. But what does that really look like? Well first you grab a teammate and make a team, this is completely free and all it requires is a Rocket League account that is linked on Rocket League Tracker Network that has placed in ranked 2v2 (this is how we get your intial rank), and a Discord account (so you can communicate with your opponents to schedule your matches).

Once you create a team on our site it will be given a rank based off the rosters collective competetive ranks. This rank is specific to that team and you will be relegated or promoted as a team based on your performances in Underground leagues. After that you head to your team page and join the queue, this is specific to your team rank/region, once the queue is full (8 teams) a league will be generated. A league consists of seven matches that must be played within an eight weeks, starting from the day the league is generated. We suggest starting from the top of your match list and working your way down but, to make the competition more flexible, it is up to you who and when you play.

Scheduling is between you and your opponent, find a time that works for everyone and afterwards head back to the website to submit the scores. A league is complete after eight weeks OR once all scores have been submitted. At this time a single elimination playoff bracket will be generated based off the standings from the season. Here you will be given another match page and you repeat the same process before.

Once the playoffs are complete or you have been eliminated from the competition you can join the queue and start again right away! The Underground is built to be automated so your not waiting for arbitrary dates or for admin to generate matches or brackets for you. In fact there is nothing stopping an entire season from being completed in a day except schedule conflicts. If you would like to have your own custom league through our site please reach out to Finalben7 on discord.

Using smurf accounts makes the game a lot less fair and fun for the majority of the players. For this reason we will be implementing bans for anyone found to be using a smurf account on our website. To avoid accidental bans we suggest using the account with the most hours or your highest rank achieved. If you suspect another user is smurfing please DM us on discord and we will look into it. As we are all aware, this is a controversial subject and often tricky to tell so please refrain from submitting a complaint unless you have found some concrete evidence.

Ban's will be put in place immediately and all matches involving the team will be given a forfeit.

To create an account click on the Sign Up button in the top right hand corner. Here you will be asked for the necessary information to create a unique account on our site. The email address, username and discord provided must be unique and not already associated with an Underground account. As mention in the signup form your Underground username MUST match the in game account you will be using during your Undergound matches AND be link on . This is so that we can get an average of your rank and place your team with other teams of similiar rank.

Email: This will be used for account verification purposes and resetting your password in the event you forget it.

Discord: We have hopes to one day implement a feature that will allows you to communicate with your opponent on the match page but for now we will be using discord.
