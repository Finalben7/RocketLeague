Frequently Asked Questions

The Underground was created to bring exciting and meaningful season based competitions, that only high end organizations currently have access to, to the everyday ranked warriors. But what does that really look like? Well first you grab a teammate and make a team, this is completely free and all it requires is a Rocket League account that is linked on Rocket League Tracker Network that has placed in ranked 2v2 (this is how we get your intial rank), and a Discord account (so you can communicate with your opponents to schedule your matches).

Once you create a team on our site it will be given a rank based off the rosters collective competetive ranks. This rank is specific to that team and you will be relegated or promoted as a team based on your performances in Underground leagues. After that you head to your team page and join the queue, this is specific to your team rank/region, once the queue is full (8 teams) a league will be generated. A league consists of seven matches that must be played within an eight weeks, starting from the day the league is generated. We suggest starting from the top of your match list and working your way down but, to make the competition more flexible, it is up to you who and when you play.

Scheduling is between you and your opponent, find a time that works for everyone and afterwards head back to the website to submit the scores. A league is complete after eight weeks OR once all scores have been submitted. At this time a single elimination playoff bracket will be generated based off the standings from the season. Here you will be given another match page and you repeat the same process before.
